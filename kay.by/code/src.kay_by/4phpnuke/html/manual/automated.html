Automated Stories


This is one of the best options of PHP-Nuke, you have the power to program your news publication at a given date.

this section is exactly the same of New Article with the difference that you can select the day, month, year, hour and minutes that you want to publish your news.

This would be very useful if you want to leave your site alone for a few days to assist to a conference, visit your parents, or just take a break or a little vacations. So, you will be enjoy this cool option for sure!
